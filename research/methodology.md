# BrandFontsIQ Research Methodology

## Overview

This document outlines the research foundation and methodology for BrandFontsIQ's approach to quantifying typography impact on business metrics.

## Research Foundation

The BrandFontsIQ methodology is based on established research in several domains:

### Web Performance Impact

Our performance impact calculations are based on research by Google and SOASTA that established a correlation between page load times and user behavior:

- 0.1 second improvement in page load time can increase conversion rates by up to 8%
- 53% of mobile site visits are abandoned if pages take longer than 3 seconds to load
- As page load time increases from 1s to 5s, probability of bounce increases by 90%

### Font Performance Factors

Typography impacts web performance in several quantifiable ways:

- Font file size affects page load time (average font file is 20KB-250KB)
- External font hosting introduces additional network requests (20-50ms per request)
- Font format efficiency varies significantly (WOFF2 is ~30% smaller than WOFF)
- Font loading strategies impact perceived performance and user experience

### Business Impact Research

Our business impact models are informed by research on customer experience:

- Harvard Business Review research showing emotionally connected customers are 52% more valuable than highly satisfied customers
- Studies indicating that brand perception can be improved by up to 13% through optimized typography
- Research showing consistent brand experience can increase revenue by 10-15%

## Methodology Development

Our methodology development process includes:

1. **Research Synthesis**: Analyzing and integrating findings from multiple research sources
2. **Model Development**: Creating mathematical models that translate font metrics to business impacts
3. **Conservative Adjustment**: Applying conservative factors to account for real-world variability
4. **Validation Planning**: Designing approaches to validate predictions against actual outcomes

## Planned Research Extensions

To further refine our methodology, we plan to:

1. Conduct industry-specific research to develop vertical-specific models
2. Partner with academic institutions for additional validation studies
3. Implement A/B testing capabilities to measure actual impact
4. Develop machine learning models to improve prediction accuracy over time

*Note: This methodology is under continuous development and will be refined as new research becomes available.*
